Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about Turbo VPN and using a VPN on PC.

What does a VPN do?

A VPN creates an encrypted connection between your device and the internet, helping protect your data and privacy on any network.

Is Turbo VPN easy to use?

Yes. Open the app, pick a region, and connect with a single tap. You can switch servers anytime.

Can I use it on public Wi‑Fi?

Absolutely. Using a VPN on public Wi‑Fi helps shield your traffic from others on the same network.

Does a VPN change my IP address?

Yes. Your traffic is routed through the VPN server, so websites and apps see the server’s IP, not your local one.

Will it slow down my internet?

VPN encryption adds a small overhead, but choosing a nearby server often keeps speeds fast and responsive.
